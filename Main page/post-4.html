The bolly songs for your next wedding dance!

Here are some bollywood songs that can enlighten your wedding party!

Posted by Saisrushty Ladegaam , Team Inaayat on 10-4-2016

No matter how much we go ‘Awww’ for Hollywood movie weddinf, the real entertainment comes only in Indian wedding, where you’ve a chance to rock it off and steal the thunders be it in your mindriff barring lehengas or showing off your amazing braiding skills and royal and elegant kurtas and sherwanis!!!
Desi Indian shaadi’s are full on entertainment packages where you find the whole family from both the sides including their ‘DHOOR KE RISHTEDAAR’!!! The best part is all the cousins, who are grown u together, bestpartners in crime ever since the childhood come up and make it a celebration. For them, a cousins marriage is the timeto have a lot of fun.
We find some typical kinds of cousins at every Indian wedding like show-off one, excited one’ fun loving one, lazy one, jealous one, complaining one, and finally the self-obsessed one!!!It’s undoubtedly a great feeling to have all of them around.
The most importantly! Grooving to the peppy Bollywood numbers! Sometimes , the hardest part of that is just picking the right song! So we’re making it easier for you!!!
HERE ARE SOME IDEAS FOR SHAKING IT OFF IN BOLLY STYLE!!!

Dilli waali Girlfriend




This is the perfect number for the first and fast ones! It is soo much funto dance on this song!!! Make the way Ranbir and Deepika completely rocked it!




Bole Chudiya




This might be the old classic, starring, gorgeous Kareena Kapoor Khan, but is an all time favourite one!!!All the ladies in the wedding can dedicate this song to their hubbies by tapping to it!




London thumkada




This is he new wedding sensational song with all the essentials in it! Starring Queen ‘Kangana ‘ already being the shaadi nazaara songs, it becomes the compulsory choice at the wedding1 As it includes both the slow and fast beats, everybody at the wedding can dance on it.




Banno tera swagger




Put some glasses on girls and you’ll sure fill this song at the wedding! This could be a perfect song for the bride and her girls! Irrspective of age, this song can make everyone feel they’ve that ‘Desi feel’ in them.




Punjabi Wedding song




I like dandiya! I like garba! But if you really wanna party! Throw your hands up and twist them around! Do the bhangra everybody! Come on do the bhangra everybody! Already being the wedding song, it boosts up the energy and emotion of the people at the wedding! The lyrics perfectly describes a wedding, and is a best one to choose!




Raadha




Three years of this song!Still is the top of the must haves on a shaadi playlist! With the best background scores, this song is the favourite pick of all the youngsters at the wedding who want to be the star to steal the show.




Mehendi lagake Rakhna




This is the ever-green song of shahrukh and kajol! Which never fails to touch the right chord of mirthful teasing and flirtation that is underway in a wedding It is also the best one for the couple to perform! And enhance the fun.




Ainvayi Ainvayi




It is the hot favourite song of everyone, ever since it is released! This song can be best for both boys and girls at wedding! It has both fun side and dancing elements in it! So any one can shake a leg on!




Raat ke Dhaai Baje




It is a song with complete wedding essence!!! Without any prior preparations, you can get on to the floor with fun beats! In the movie, the song has special ‘Lanvi’ step, which when practised can add extra colours and attention to the performance!

*Its just how you can enjoy the wedding, how you get exposed to everyone, At this point, nobody judges you how you dance.
